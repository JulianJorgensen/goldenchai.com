Manifesto #2: Your users are the primary focus: Intuitively guided, they should achieve their goal with least effort.

Web users are extremely impatient. Any redundant click, or effort using your site, distracts them from achieving their goal. Within seconds they may give up and click way to your competition.

To keep the users' focus, only highly applicable information need be presented. Utilizing randomized A/B tests, the user experience, and their focus is continuously optimized.

This leads to a higher Conversion Rate, which means we've given the right content to the right user at the right time.
